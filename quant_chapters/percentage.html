Question 1

Price of an Iphone was $200 which was 20% of your pocket money. What is your pocket money?

Explanation

Step 1: Let the pocket money be $X.

Step 2: Iphone price is 20% of $X ==> 20*X/100 = $200.

Step 3: ==> X= $1000

Question 2

A Student got 30% of the maximum marks and failed by 10 marks. If maximum marks are 150 find the minimum marks required to pass the exam?

Explanation

Step 1: The student got 30% of the maximum marks, ==> 30% of 150 ==> 0.3*150 ==> 45

Step 2: It is said that 10 more marks are needed for the particular student to pass.

Step 3: ==> Passing marks = marks of given student + 10 ==> 45 + 10 ==> 55

Question 3

An increase of 83.33% in Mark's salary after which his salary becomes 12K. What was his initial salary?

Explanation

Step 1: Assume initial salary of mark was $X.

Step 2: After increase in salary his new salary becomes ==> X + 83.33% of X ==> 183.33% of X.

Step 3: This 183.33% of X is equal to 12K according to the question.

Step 4: ==> 1.8333 * X = 12000

Step 5: ==> X = $6545.5

Question 4

If radius of circle is increased by 40% then what is the % change in area of circle?

Explanation

Step 1: Let the radius os circle be r.

Step 2: The area of circle initially is pi * r^2

Step 3: Now the radius is increased by 40% ==> r + 40% of r = 1.4r

Step 4: ==> Area of new circle = 1.96 pi * r^2

Step 5: ==> % increase in area = [(1.96-1)/1] pi*r^2 = 96% increase

Question 5

Amit has 12.5% share value less than Bhavesh, then Bhavesh's share value is what % more than Amit's?

Explanation

Step 1: As we have no information about anyones share in absolute terms we can assume one of the person's share anything that we want.

Step 2: Let Bhavesh's share be $100.

Step 3: Amit's share = 100 - 12.5% of 100 ==> 87.5

Step 4: % Bhavesh's share is more than Amit's = (100 - 87.5)/87.5

Step 5: % Bhavesh's share is more than Amit's = 14.3%

Question 6

The rate of inflation in country is 10 % p.a.. Cost of an Article is 6$ now then what will be the cost of the article two year from now?

Explanation

Step 1: Price after first year = 6 + 10% of 6 = $6.6

Step 2: Price after second year = 6.6 + 10% of 6.6 = $7.26

Generalization, For n years = Initial Price * (1 + r/100)^n

Question 7

When a positive number x is multiplied by the same number then resulting number is what percent more than the original number.

Explanation

Step 1: Let the number be x

Step 2: After multiplying with itself the number becomes x*x

Step 3: % increase in number = (x^2 - x)/x = (x-1)*100 %
