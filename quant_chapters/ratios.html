Question 1

If the ratio of 4 angles in a quadrilateral is 2:7:8:7.Then find the angle B.

Explanation

Step 1: Let the common ratio be x.

Step 2: ==> A = 2x B = 7x C = 8x D = 7x

Step 3: ==> A + B + C + D = 360

Step 4: ==> x = 15

Step 5: ==> B = 15 * 7 ==> 105

Question 2

The number of Apple to banana to kiwi is 2:5:11 and the number of banana is 45 more than the number of apples. How many bananas are there?

Explanation

Step 1: Let common ratio be x.

Step 2: ==> Apples + 45 = banana ==> 2x + 45 = 5x ==> x = 15

Step 3: ==> Number of bananas is 5x ==> 5*15 = 75

Question 3

A mixture contains milk and water in the ratio of 3:1. The volume of the mixture is 60 litres. How much water should be added to make the ratio of milk to water 2:1?

Explanation

Step 1: Let common ratio be x.

Step 2: ==> 3x + x = 60 ==> x = 15

Step 3: There is 45 litres of milk and 15 litres of water in the original mixture.

Step 4: Now some water is added such that the ratio becomes 2:1. The amount of milk does not change.

Step 5: Suppose V litres of water was added to mixture. ==> Now volume of mixture becomes 60 + V.

Step 6: Now lets find the milk in the final mixture. ==> 2/3 (60 + V)

Step 7: Because milk has not changed we can equate the amount of milk before to amount of milk in the final mixture.

Step 8: 45 = 2/3 * (60 + V)

Step 9: ==> V = 7.5

Question 4

5 Years ago,ages of father and son was in the ratio 7:2. Four years hence ratio of there age will be 3:1. Find present age of son.

Explanation

Step 1: Let current age of father be F and son be S.

Step 2: Five years ago==> Age of father = F - 5 and Age of Son = S - 5

Step 3: As given in the question, (F-5)/(S-5) = 7/2 ==> 2F = 7S - 25

Step 4: After 4 years from the present ==> Age of father = F + 4 and Age of Son = S + 4

Step 5: As given in question, (F-4)/(S-4) = 3/1 ==> F = 3S - 8

Step 6: now there are 2 equations and 2 variables so solve for S.
